Who I am

Products built for

Originally from Ethiopia, I came to the United States and built a record I'm proud of — fast. I'm a junior at The George Washington University studying Information Systems, graduating December 2026, and I care deeply about making technology that is accessible, human, and useful.

I've worked with News Corp diagnosing why Gen-Z users were disengaging from AI features, with PwC helping a nonprofit rethink how it raises funding, and independently built an AI-powered shopping product from scratch. I don't wait to be handed the right conditions — I build the conditions.

I'm fluent in English, native in Tigrinya, and conversational in Amharic. Outside work, I'm drawn to edtech, fintech, consumer products, and anything where good product thinking can make something meaningfully better for the people using it.
